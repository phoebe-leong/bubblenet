What is AnonTwit?

AnonTwit stands for Anonymous Twitter. It's a place where anyone can anonymously put out short messages, similar to Twitter.
It's very simple and is in the very early stages of development.

By design, AnonTwit has no user infrastructure, no profiles can be created. This means it is completely anonymous.
It was made to be used as a way to anonymously express thoughts, feelings, and ideas, becoming some sort of a speech melting pot.

There will eventually be post moderation.
