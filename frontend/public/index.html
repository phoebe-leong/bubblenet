What is AnonTwit?

AnonTwit stands for Anonymous Twitter. Originally, it was thought of as a place where anyone can anonymously put out short messages, similar to Twitter, but with the anonymity of 4chan.

This was the thought that I ran with for most of the site's development up until recently. Through testing during development, I have found that AnonTwit functions best
as a bulletin board for small, enclosed communities, such as a household or a school camp. Hereon, it will be designed primarily for LANs, not for exposure to the outside world.

By design, AnonTwit has no user infrastructure, no profiles can be created. This means it is completely anonymous.
It was made to be used as a way to anonymously express thoughts, feelings, and ideas, becoming some sort of a speech melting pot.

There will eventually be post moderation. Click the site name at the top of the page to access the feed.
